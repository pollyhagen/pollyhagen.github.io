Aberdeen University Canoe Club has pool sessions at the shiny new pool in the Aberdeen Aquatic Centre every Tuesday and Thursday at 9pm-10:30pm and 7:30pm-9pm respectively for general skills and a session on a Sunday evening at 7:30 for those looking to develop their canoe polo skills. We are an extremely active club with trips to local rivers such as the Dee and the North Esk running every weekend and several trips further afield every term; as well as partaking in a variety of competitions throughout the year and continuing to be one of the most socially active clubs at Aberdeen University.
