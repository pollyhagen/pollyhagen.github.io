Captain: Jack Brickell

Jack Brickell is a spectacularly fluffy individual who secured the post of Captain by means of an alcohol induced promise to eat a pine cone. He’s a hairy hipster who came to us from a background of sprint kayaking and is therefore full of stylish moves despite often looking like the most scared individual on the river. Off the water, you might find Jack gallivanting in Invergarry with various ‘big deals’, nurturing his love affair for Canada, hipstering it up at a gig in suitably loud clothing or running around in the wild looking at rocks- always with an arsenal of sarcastic replies and quotes from various obscure films. Although this year it is more likely that his time will be consumed encouraging and babysitting the liability that is AUCC.

Vice-Captain: Kitty Birley

She’s AUCC offspring so kayaking literally runs in her blood. You can tell this due to the big grin on her face when she comes out of a horrible swim; even those that meant she got the prestigious swim of the year! It’s hard for her enthusiasm not to rub off on you. Whether it be on the river or while drinking, Kitty is there to support us and show us how it’s done. This includes but is not limited to pushing people into a frozen canal to get the ball in polo or getting kicked out of a bar while leading confused freshers around Aberdeen. She’s the one you can blame for the fabulousness of Fresher’s weekend and the one you can thank for keeping you smiling when it’s not quite going your way. All that and an extra sprinkle of weirdness will make her a great Vice Captain.

Treasurer: Mitchell Roper

Mitchell Roper is our resident Canadian eh! His hair is long and his heart is big. He loves nothing more than throwing himself down the mighty rivers of Scotland, usually upside down, and can often be spotted in Tunnels with a suspicious glint in his eye. Mitchell is not only handsome but he is in charge of our money and is a medic! A lot of responsibility for the real life Trailer Park Boy but you can be sure he’ll give you a good deal on any trips!

Secretary: Michaela Howett

Michaela joined the club a shy, innocent fresher with the alcohol tolerance of a 12 year old. Three years later and it’s safe to say she hasn't lost her drinking ability, but her innocence? You can judge that for yourself... A night out with Michaela always ends with a story, from strangers mistaking her spew for bolognaise to climbing abandoned towers at 4am in the freezing cold, it will be entertaining whatever the outcome! As our secretary she will be looking after our website as well as our blog - an ideal job for the literary competence of an English student!

Events Officer: Keira Lockie-Anrig

Keira, a goddess greater than mermaids or unicorns, was delivered to AUCC from a land of love, kindness and all things hippy! No matter what, our lovely lady always has a beaming smile on her face (even when teamed with bumps, cuts and blood!) and with her hardcore enthusiasm from the word go, it was inevitable that she would become one of our events officers/ In her role you will see her kindly encouraging you to all of our epic events and after pool socials, providing massive smiles on the river, drunk as a skunk and probably walking around with no shoes on. However, she does come with one caution label, as she possesses similar traits to a venus fly trap which some of you may experience on our endless nights out!

Events Officer: Joe Schamp

Holding most of canoe club’s values to heart such as a general disregard for his own heath and well-being and an overwhelming commitment to adventuring (don’t ask about his gap yahhh, but do ask about exploring elephants sexually) it made sense that Joe be responsible for making sure that every event AUCC holds will leave you wondering what happened.
